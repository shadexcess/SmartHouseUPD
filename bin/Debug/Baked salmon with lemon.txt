 And here is the recipe for the baked salmon with lemons.

 Ingredients:
 Salmon fillet
 Olive oil
 Lemon
 Garlic
 Salt and pepper
 Fresh parsley

 Steps:
 1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit.
 2. Prepare the salmon. 
 Pat the salmon dry and season it well with kosher salt. 
 Place the salmon on a sheet of foil on a baking sheet.
 3. Prepare the topping. Drizzle the salmon with olive oil. 
 Sprinkle it with garlic, salt, and pepper. 
 Top it with slices of lemon and fresh parsley.
 4. Bake the salmon. Fold the foil around the salmon to make a packet. 
 Bake it in the preheated oven for about 18-21 minutes, until the salmon flakes easily with a fork.

 Enjoy your meal!
