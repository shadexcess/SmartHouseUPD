 And here is the recipe for the fresh salad with apples and peaches.

 Ingredients:
 2 peaches
 2 apples
 200g white grape
 1 lime
 2 tbsp sugar
 2 sprigs of basil

 Steps:
 1. Wash all fruits thoroughly.
 2. Slice the peaches, cut the apples into cubes, and cut each grape in halves.
 3. In a salad bowl, mix the fruit, zest, and juice. Add chopped basil leaves.
 4. Refrigerate for an hour to let the juices come out and blend together.

 Enjoy your meal!
